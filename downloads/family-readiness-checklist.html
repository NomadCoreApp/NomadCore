Family Readiness Checklist

Check off each item as you complete it. Review every 6 months.

Emergency Plan

  • Written emergency plan that all family members know
  • Designated family meeting point (near home)
  • Secondary meeting point (outside neighborhood)
  • Out-of-area emergency contact assigned
  • Emergency contact numbers memorized or on cards
  • Plan reviewed/practiced in the last 6 months

Emergency Supplies

  • Go-bag packed and accessible
  • 3+ days of water (1 gallon/person/day)
  • 3+ days of non-perishable food
  • Flashlight + extra batteries
  • First aid kit (stocked and current)
  • Battery-powered or hand-crank radio
  • Phone charger (portable battery pack)
  • Cash in small bills
  • Prescription medications (7-day supply)
  • Supplies rotated/checked in last 6 months

Critical Documents

  • IDs (driver's license, passport) — copies secured
  • Insurance policies — copies secured
  • Medical records & prescriptions — copies secured
  • Financial account information — copies secured
  • Property deeds / lease agreements — copies secured
  • Digital backup stored securely (encrypted)
  • Physical copies in go-bag or fireproof safe

Home Safety

  • Know how to shut off gas
  • Know how to shut off water main
  • Know how to shut off electricity (breaker panel)
  • Smoke detectors tested (monthly)
  • Fire extinguisher accessible and current
  • Evacuation route from every room identified

Evacuation Readiness

  • Primary evacuation route planned
  • Alternate evacuation route planned
  • Destination identified (hotel, family, shelter)
  • Vehicle gas tank kept above half
  • Pet supplies and carrier ready
  • Important items pre-staged near exit

Skills & Training

  • Someone trained in basic first aid
  • Someone trained in CPR
  • Kids know how to call 911
  • Family has practiced an emergency drill
  • Know when to shelter-in-place vs. evacuate
