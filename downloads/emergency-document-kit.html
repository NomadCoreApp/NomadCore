Emergency Document Kit

Secure copies of everything that matters. Update every 6 months.

Personal Identification

  • Driver's license / state ID — copy secured
  • Passport — copy secured
  • Social Security cards — copy secured
  • Birth certificates — copy secured
  • Marriage / divorce certificates — copy secured
  • Adoption / custody papers — copy secured

Financial Records

  • Bank account numbers and contact info
  • Credit card numbers and company phone numbers
  • Investment / retirement account details
  • Mortgage / loan documents
  • Tax returns (last 2 years)
  • Safe deposit box location and key info

Insurance Policies

  • Homeowner's / renter's insurance — copy secured
  • Auto insurance — copy secured
  • Health insurance cards — copy secured
  • Life insurance — copy secured
  • Policy numbers and agent contact info written down

Medical Records

  • Current prescriptions list with dosages
  • Allergies and medical conditions documented
  • Doctor and pharmacy contact information
  • Vaccination records
  • Medical power of attorney

Property & Legal

  • Property deed / lease agreement — copy secured
  • Vehicle titles — copy secured
  • Will / trust documents — copy secured
  • Power of attorney documents
  • Pet ownership / vaccination records

Digital Backup

  • All documents scanned to digital format
  • Stored in encrypted cloud backup or app
  • USB drive copy in fireproof safe or go-bag
  • Backup updated within last 6 months
  • Shared access with trusted family member
