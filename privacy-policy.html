NomadCore Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority: NomadCore operates entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

1. Data Collection

NomadCore is designed with privacy at its core. The app operates entirely offline, meaning we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

2. Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods, including:

  • Emergency contacts (ICE contacts)
  • Inventory items (PackMind)
  • Location preferences
  • Custom procedures
  • Personal profile information
  • Emergency plans
  • Weather locations

3. Permissions

NomadCore may request the following permissions:

  • Location: Used only for weather updates and finding nearby emergency resources. Location data never leaves your device.
  • Storage: Required to save your data locally on your device.
  • Camera: Only for QR code scanning in emergency plan sharing (optional feature).
  • Phone: To enable quick dialing of emergency contacts and 911.

4. Third-Party Services

When online, NomadCore may connect to:

  • OpenWeatherMap: For weather data (no personal information is shared, only location coordinates)
  • MapTiler: For offline map downloads (no tracking or personal data)

5. Data Sharing

We never share, sell, or transmit your personal data. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (such as sharing emergency plans via QR code with trusted contacts).

6. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security measures. We recommend using device lock screens and keeping your operating system updated.

7. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. You can delete all your data at any time through:

Settings > Data Management > Delete All Data

Or by uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

NomadCore does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in emergency preparedness.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Check the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page for the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Information

For privacy concerns or questions, please contact us through:

  • Website: nomad-core.com
