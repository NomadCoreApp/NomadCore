Our Story

We built this because we needed it

"In 2024, Texas got hit hard. Storms. Hurricanes. Floods. Power out for days. I found myself searching for an app that could tell me what to do and actually work when the internet didn't. I found plenty of apps. None of them worked offline. Most wanted subscriptions. All of them wanted my data. So I built what I was looking for."

NomadCore is built by a product development engineer in the medical device industry and a parent who believes your family's safety information shouldn't depend on a cell tower.

When "AI-powered" usually means "sends your data to corporate servers," we made a different choice: locally-stored, verified data that works without internet and stays on your device.
