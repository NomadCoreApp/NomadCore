Delete Your Account

Permanently remove your NomadCore account and all associated data

⚠️

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all of your data will be permanently removed from our servers. You will not be able to recover your account or any associated data.

What gets deleted

When you delete your NomadCore account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Account profile
  • Cloud backups
  • Family group memberships
  • Shared family data
  • Bulletin posts & comments
  • Reactions & acknowledgments
  • Calendar events & RSVPs
  • Group inventory items
  • Maintenance schedules
  • Location sharing data
  • Push notification tokens
  • Analytics & audit logs

What stays on your device

Your local data (emergency plans, documents, offline maps, supply lists) will remain on your device until you uninstall the app. Account deletion only removes cloud-synced data from our servers.

Prefer to delete from within the app?

You can also delete your account directly from the NomadCore app:

  1. Open NomadCore and go to Settings
  2. Scroll down to Account & Profile
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm by typing DELETE and entering your email

Request Account Deletion

Enter the email address associated with your NomadCore account. We will verify your identity and process the deletion.

Need help?

If you're having trouble with your account or have questions before deleting, contact us at support@nomad-core.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Account deletion requests are processed within 48 hours. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been removed.
