The documents you can't afford to lose

After Hurricane Katrina, hundreds of thousands of people couldn't prove who they were, what they owned, or what insurance they had. FEMA claims were delayed for months — sometimes years.

Quick question: If you had to evacuate in 10 minutes, could you grab all of these?

☐ Passports and birth certificates
☐ Insurance policies (home, auto, health)
☐ Property deeds or mortgage docs
☐ Prescriptions and medical records
☐ Wills and power of attorney

If you hesitated on any of those, this week's guide is for you. It covers the full document kit — what to include, how to organize it, and why you need both physical and digital copies.
